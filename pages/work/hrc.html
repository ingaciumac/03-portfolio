Human Rights Communication 2.0

Client

OSCE(PDIHR)

Project Description

The overall objective of the event is to raise awareness among human rights defenders about innovative approaches on human rights communication and build capacity of human rights defenders to promote human rights effectively by employing various and up-to-date communication tools and skills.
My role was to design an original concept derived from ODIHR branding with distinctive elements that can be used where necessary, digital and print during the event and in the period of promoting the event. To prepare the set of digital products used for communication and branding of content in accordance with event format and target audience: event poster, keynote presentation design, social media set of branding, static web banners, pdf program of the event, etc. To prepare the set of print materials used during the event: stage design, branded banners, official badge, event signs, screen preview for speakers & topic, official announcements for breaks and other helpful information.
